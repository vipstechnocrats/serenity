Promoting Mental Health

Sustainable Development Goal 3 (SDG 3) aims to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages. Among its key targets is the promotion of mental health and well-being, recognizing that mental health is a critical component of overall health.

Importance of Mental Health

Mental health encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how we think, feel, and act, affecting our ability to handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Promoting mental health is essential for the development of individuals and communities.

Targets for Mental Health

  • Target 3.4: Reduce premature mortality from non-communicable diseases and promote mental health and well-being.
  • Target 3.5: Strengthen the prevention and treatment of substance abuse, including narcotic drug and harmful use of alcohol.


Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I experience a panic attack?

If you experience a panic attack, try to focus on your breathing and remind yourself that the attack will pass. Practice grounding techniques, such as focusing on your surroundings or holding an object. Seek help from a healthcare provider if the panic attacks are frequent or severe.

How can I manage stress effectively?

Effective stress management can involve techniques such as regular exercise, mindfulness meditation, deep breathing exercises, and time management strategies. It may also be helpful to identify and address the sources of stress in your life.

When should I seek help for depression?

You should seek help for depression if you experience persistent feelings of sadness, loss of interest in activities, or if your symptoms interfere with daily functioning. If you have thoughts of self-harm or suicide, seek immediate help from a mental health professional or crisis hotline.

Are there support groups for anxiety and depression?

Yes, there are many support groups available for those struggling with anxiety and depression. These can provide valuable peer support and coping strategies. You can find support groups through local mental health organizations, online forums, and community centers.
